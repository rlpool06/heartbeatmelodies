How does it work?

Are you looking for a truly unique way to remember the season of expecting a child? Let us help you! We take an audio sample of your baby's heartbeat and turn it into a song!

RECORD THE HEART BEAT

Grab a quick 30-60 second audio recording of your baby's heartbeat. You can do this at your doctor appointment or with an at-home monitor.

SEND IT IN

Fill out our order form and send in your audio sample. We'll get to work on creating your song! Once we finish a first draft, we'll send it to you for review and final touches.

ENJOY AND SHARE

It's here! It's time to enjoy your song at home, share it with friends, even show it off at your baby shower.
